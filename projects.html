Blender

I have always loved design ever since I was a little kid, and once when I was on Youtube I saw a video 'Blender Beginner Tutorial' by Blender Guru. Now I had heard of Blender before, but I never tried to learn how to use it before this video. After toying around in Blender for awhile, I needed a project to do that would really push me to learn more about 3D modelling.


Creating a city


For what seemed like perfect timing, I had to choose a 'Personal Project' for Grade 10 in the IB. I ended up choosing to make a futuristic cyberpunk-style city in Blender, as it would be a large scale project with a deadline. After about 10 hours or so, I realized I really enjoyed what I was doing. The creative freedom I had during this project was a huge motivation for me putting around 130 hours into the project, which was over 100 hours over the recommended time. Overall, I am really proud of how it turned out.
